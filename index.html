What are the types of Bone Marrow Transplant?

Autologuous Bone Marrow Transplant-Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body andtransfused back after high dose chemotherapy.

Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling Donor)-Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a gentically(HLA) matched sibling donar.

Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Unrelated Donor)-Replacement of diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a genetically(HLA) matched donor obtained from bone marrow registries in India and abroad.

Haplo-identical Bone Marrow Transplant-Reaplacement diseased or defective marrow with marrow from the parent.

Umbilical cord Bone Marrow thransplat-Replacement Of diseasedor defective marrow from a genetically(HLA)matched umbilical cord donar obtained from umbilical cord registries in India and abroad.

What are the conditions that requireBone Marrow Transplant?

BMT is the treatment of choice for blood disorder like Thalassemia,bone marrow failure syndromes like Aplastic anemia,serious immune deficiencies and certain generic metabolic disorders.

It is also used to treat high risk cancers like some cases of Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia.Acute MYeloid Leukemia.Multiple MYeloma and solid tumours like neuroblastoma.Ewings Sarcoma and certain relapsed cancers like Lymphomas
